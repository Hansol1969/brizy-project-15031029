ABOUT

Established in 2021

Our company was established in 2021 and is continuously growing. When it comes to electrical installations, we do everything we can to make your dreams come to life. We can help you with anything from completely new installations to renovations or fixing of deviations that are discovered during inspections.We have got you covered for lighting, fuse boxes, electrical car chargers, floor heating and much more.

All of our services are performed in accordance with current building regulations in Norway. We have all the qualifications from Direktoratet for samfunnssikkerhet og beredskap (DSB) which are necessary to run an electrical business in Norway.

All of our employees have extensive experience in the industry which means we can handle even the most difficult projects. We use materials from trusted suppliers, which means we deliver the highest quality services at a reasonable price. Our main aim is to make your home a comfortable and safe place for you and your family.
